Project Title: EmpowerH

1. Problem Statement
Across societies, individuals face persistent challenges such as sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV), domestic abuse, and mental health struggles like depression, trauma, and anxiety. Victims often remain silent due to shame, fear of judgment, cultural stigma, and lack of accessible support systems.
At the same time, communities lack effective awareness, response, and reporting mechanisms, leaving survivors without timely help, while misinformation and stigma worsen their struggles.
There is a critical need for an inclusive, secure, and accessible platform that empowers individuals to:
Report violence safely and confidentially.


Access professional mental health support.


Build awareness and learn about their rights.


Connect with trusted community resources.


2. Project Objectives
The main objectives of the app are to:
Provide a confidential and anonymous reporting system for victims of sexual, gender-based, and domestic violence.


Offer mental health support services, including access to professionals, peer groups, and AI-guided self-care tools.


Create awareness campaigns within the app to educate users on violence prevention, mental health, and legal rights.


Build a resource hub that connects victims to NGOs, legal aid, health services, and emergency contacts.


Foster a supportive online community where people can share experiences, receive encouragement, and find hope.



3. Target Users
Victims & Survivors of sexual, gender-based, and domestic violence.


Individuals struggling with mental health issues such as depression, anxiety, and trauma.


Youth and students facing social pressure, bullying, or stress.


Community members who want to learn and raise awareness.


Organisations, NGOs, and mental health professionals offering support services.



4. Key Features
Anonymous Reporting System


Secure, confidential reporting channels.


Option to connect reports directly to support organisations or law enforcement (with user consent).


Mental Health Support


24/7 AI-driven chat for initial guidance.


Access to certified counsellors and psychologists.


Peer support groups and safe discussion forums.


Resource Hub


Directory of NGOs, legal services, shelters, and hotlines.


Educational content on rights, self-protection, and well-being.


Community & Awareness


Articles, videos, and podcasts to break stigma.


Awareness campaigns and virtual events.


User stories to inspire hope and healing.


Emergency Help


Quick “SOS” button to contact local emergency services.


Geolocation-enabled emergency response (optional).



5. Expected Impact
Empower survivors by giving them safe and accessible ways to speak up.


Reduce stigma surrounding mental health and violence through awareness.


Improve response time for victims seeking urgent help.


Strengthen community resilience by building trust, support networks, and knowledge.



6. Long-Term Vision
The app aims to evolve into a comprehensive social well-being ecosystem, addressing not just violence and mental health, but also youth empowerment, family counselling, and community development. It will serve as a bridge between individuals, professionals, and institutions, ensuring that no one suffers in silence.

## Introduction

Welcome to EmpowerH, a comprehensive web platform dedicated to addressing gender-based violence against women and promoting women's wellness. 
This project is the result of collaborative efforts by a team of passionate developers committed to leveraging technology for social good


## Project Overview

EmpowerH provides a robust platform where women can connect, share experiences, and engage in discussions on crucial topics. The platform features a Women's Wellness Hub that offers health tips and reminders, encouraging users to adopt healthy lifestyles. Key thematic areas include:

1. Gender-Based Violence Awareness: Educational resources and awareness campaigns aimed at combating gender-based violence.

2. Sexual Awareness in Girl Child: Information and resources focused on empowering and protecting young girls.

3. Resources Awareness Page: A centralized repository of support services and legal resources for women in need.

4. Women's Wellness Hub: Subscription-based health and wellness tips to encourage users to maintain healthy lifestyles.
